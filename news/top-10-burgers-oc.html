Top 10 Burgers in Orange County

The ultimate OC burger list — from smash burgers to gourmet stacks locals swear by.

#1 Peter’s Gourmade Grill (Tustin)

Widely considered one of the best overall burgers in OC, known for juicy patties, bold seasoning, and consistently high-quality builds.

#2 Hammer Burger (Santa Ana)

A smash burger favorite with strong local hype, crispy edges, and simple but addictive flavor.

#3 The Cut Handcrafted Burgers (Irvine)

Gourmet-style burgers with elevated toppings and premium ingredients.

#4 TK Burgers (Multiple OC Locations)

Old-school, fast, and reliable — a classic beachside burger experience.

#5 In-N-Out Burger

The most iconic fast burger in California. Simple, consistent, and always fresh.

#6 Eureka! (Irvine / Newport)

Craft burgers with creative toppings and a modern gastropub feel.

#7 Habit Burger & Grill

Chargrilled flavor that stands out from typical fast food chains.

#8 Hopdoddy Burger Bar (Newport Beach)

Premium burgers with fresh ingredients and craft-style combinations.

#9 Bunz Burger Joint

Known for build-your-own style burgers and strong smash burger options.

#10 Swagyu Burger

Wagyu-focused burgers delivering a richer, more premium bite.

Final Thoughts

Orange County has one of the strongest burger scenes in California. Whether you want fast and simple or gourmet and stacked, there’s something here for every craving.

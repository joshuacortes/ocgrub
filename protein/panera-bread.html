Panera Bread Protein Guide in Orange County

Looking for healthier fast casual meals with solid protein? Panera Bread is a popular Orange County choice for grilled chicken sandwiches, salads, soups, and bowls that can fit higher-protein goals.

Popular Pick

Chicken Sandwiches

Panera offers multiple grilled chicken sandwich options that can deliver strong protein totals depending on bread and toppings.

Protein Range: ~30g to 40g+

Lean Option

Chicken Salads

Choose salads with grilled chicken for lighter calories while still getting quality protein and vegetables.

Protein Range: ~25g to 35g+

Balanced Meal

Bowls & Combos

Bowls and combo meals can combine soup, salad, or sandwich choices for a filling protein-focused lunch.

Protein Range: varies by combo

Best Protein Tips at Panera Bread

  • Add grilled chicken when available.
  • Choose salads or bowls for leaner macros.
  • Pick whole grain breads when possible.
  • Watch dressings and creamy sauces if cutting calories.
  • Use Panera nutrition tools before ordering.

Panera Bread has many Orange County locations, making it a convenient protein meal stop for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

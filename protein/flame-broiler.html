Flame Broiler

Flame Broiler is one of Southern California’s most recognized protein-focused fast casual chains. Known for grilled chicken bowls, steak plates, rice bowls, and veggie sides, it is a common Orange County choice for customers seeking filling meals with strong protein totals.

Best Protein Orders at Flame Broiler

White Meat Chicken Plate

One of the top protein options. Public nutrition listings show about 63g protein depending on preparation. Great for post-workout meals.

Chicken Bowl

Popular daily option with rice, vegetables, and grilled chicken. Nutrition listings show around 43g to 45g protein depending on sauce.

Steak Bulgogi Plate

A flavorful alternative for beef lovers with about 50g protein on many nutrition listings.

Why Flame Broiler Is Popular for Protein

Flame Broiler built its reputation on simple bowls and plates centered around grilled proteins, rice, vegetables, and lighter sauces. Customers often choose it when they want something faster than meal prep but cleaner than traditional fast food.

The Habit Burger Grill

The Habit Burger Grill is a popular California-born chain known for chargrilled burgers, grilled chicken sandwiches, fresh salads, and protein-friendly meal choices. With multiple Orange County locations, Habit is a strong option for customers wanting flavorful protein meals.

High Protein Chargrilled California Chain Chicken Options Popular Choice

Best Protein Orders at The Habit

Double Charburger

One of the highest protein burger choices on the menu with about 39g protein depending on build. Great for bigger appetites and post-workout meals.

Grilled Chicken Sandwich

A leaner option for protein-focused customers wanting grilled chicken instead of beef. Popular for balanced lunches.

Grilled Chicken Salad

Habit's grilled chicken salad has around 35g protein without dressing, making it one of the cleanest menu options.

Why The Habit Is Good for Protein

The Habit stands out because many menu items are cooked over an open flame, giving burgers and chicken a chargrilled taste. Customers can choose beef, grilled chicken, lettuce wraps, or salads depending on calorie and protein goals.
